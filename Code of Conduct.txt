This is the Code of Conduct that must be followed

The Guidelines are as follows - 

Be open and welcome any ideas regarding project. Be Inclusive.

Respect each team member even if you agree/disagree with their ideas. 

To show participation in completing assigned task to their best ability although if one cannot due to any reason be transparent and discuss with the team members in good time to avoid unpleasant situation.

Manager (or relevant person) should be aware of any adverse situations and handle them accordingly to avoid unpleasant situations amongst team members:
Any uncivil scenarios should be dealt with proper care taking into account the groups well being.

Accept own faults and mistakes and provide alternate solutions:
Be able to take accountability for mistakes and to be able to provide an answer without giving up.

Attend all meetings in-person or online (zoom, teams, skype etc...):
Make sure the team can have access to your work as well as you to be able to discuss.

All communication regarding the project is clear and written (WhatsApp...) to avoid miscommunication:
Communication is key when working on a project as a group to avoid and/or act on mistakes.

Where possible appreciate and encourage each other:
This is to produce a healthy enviornment for members to work on increasing productivity.

Show integrity:
Make sure your work is your own and to let members know of sources you may have gathered information from through references.

Come to meetings prepared and on time.

No discrmination, bullying or insults to be tolerated.

During the meetings, keep conversations work related and professional.

If you are unable to attend meetings to communicate before hand as soon as possible.

Failure to follow the Code of Conduct will result in penalties and eventually suspension.
